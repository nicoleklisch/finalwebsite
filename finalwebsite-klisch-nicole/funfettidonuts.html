Funfetti Donuts

Doughnuts have been a Sunday ritual for me since I was a little girl and my dad would take me to get one after church.  I would of course go straight to the one with the most rainbow sprinkles.  These days poor Connor gets dragged to each bakery near and far to get our hands on any doughnut we can. Today I spent my day eating these babies!  These Baked Sprinkle Doughnuts are made with almond flour and sweetened with honey so you don’t have to worry about eating more than one!

Funfetti Donuts

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ Cups of Blanched Almond Flour
  • 1 Teaspoon of Baking Soda
  • 4 Tablespoons of Raw Honey
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 3 Teaspoons of Vanilla (Split)
  • ¼¼ Teaspoon of Almond Extract
  • 2 Tablespoons of Coconut Oil
  • ¼ Cup of Rainbow Sprinkles plus extra for topping
  • 1 Cup of Powdered Sugar
  • 3 Teaspoons of Almond Milk
  1. 1. Preheat oven to 325° and spray your doughnut pan with cooking oil.
  2. 2. In a large bowl place your almond flour and baking powder and whisk to combine.
  3. 3. Melt your coconut oil in a small microwave save bowl just until its softened.
  4. 4. Add the rest of your wet ingredients in the bowl containing the coconut oil.
  5. 5. Combine your dry and wet ingredients and pour your rainbow sprinkles in the batter and gently fold in.
  6. 6. Put batter into a piping bag and pipe batter into doughnut pan, filling each cavity until its almost to the top. If you don’t have piping bag use a large plastic baggy and cut a whole in it.
  7. 7. Cook doughnuts on 325⁰ and start checking to doneness around 10 minutes.
  8. 8. Once doughnuts are cooked through, remove from oven and let cool in pan for 5 minutes. Remove doughnuts and place on cooling rack.
  9. 9. Now for the glaze. Mix your powered sugar, vanilla extract and almond milk. Dunk each cooled doughnut one by one into the glaze and douse them in the sprinkles.
  10. 10. Place doughnuts back on the cooling wrack and let the glaze set.
  11. 11. Enjoy!.
