Pineapple Pico De Gallo Salmon

I first prepared my pico de gallo.  A classic version of this is tomatoes, red onion, Serrano peppers, cilantro, lime juice, and salt.  I wanted to punch up the flavor a bit so I added some pineapple…I think this is what really had me envisioning I was on a beach.  After chopping and dicing all the ingredients, I set it aside so that the flavors could develop.

Next I took the 4 salmon fillets and patted them dry with a paper towel to remove any access moisture.  Created my rub, which was just chili powder, cumin, smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, red pepper flakes, salt, and black pepper. Sprinkled the rub on my salmon and popped it in the oven for 10-15 minutes.

Pineapple Pico de Gallo Salmon

Ingredients:

  • 4 Salmon Fillets
  • ½ Teaspoon of Chili Powder
  • ¼ Teaspoon of Cumin
  • ¼ Teaspoon of Smoked Paprika
  • ¼ Teaspoon of Onion Powder
  • ¼ Teaspoon of Garlic Powder
  • 1 Pinch of Red Pepper Flakes
  • 1 Cup of Diced Tomatoes
  • ½ Cup of Red Onion
  • ½ Cup of Pineapple
  • 1 ½ Tablespoons of Serrano Peppers
  • ¼ Cup of Cilantro
  • 1 Lime
  • ¼ Teaspoon + Pinch of Salt
  • ¼ Teaspoon of Black Pepper
  1. 1. First prepare the Pineapple Pico de Gallo.
  2. 2. Dice the tomatoes, red onion, pineapple, and Serrano peppers and place in a small bowl.
  3. 3. Chop the cilantro and place in Pico de Gallo mixture. Season with a pinch of salt.
  4. 4. Cut the lime in half and squeeze the juice on top of the Pico de Gallo mixture.
  5. 5. Set aside to let the flavors develop.
  6. 6. Preheat oven to 400°.
  7. 7. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and place the 4 salmon fillets evenly across.
  8. 8. In a small bowl mix up the salmon rub: chili powder, cumin, smoked paprika, onion powder, garlic powder, red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper.
  9. 9. Rub the seasoning all over the salmon.
  10. 10. Place the salmon in the oven and bake for 10-15 minutes.
  11. 11. Cut the remaining half of the lime into 4 wedges.
  12. 12. Once the salmon is finished, serve it up with the Pineapple Pico de Gallo and a lime wedge.
  13. 13. Enjoy!
